Why does y++ move DOWN in p5.js?

Traditional Math (Cartesian)

Origin (0,0) at bottom-left

y++ moves ↑ UP

Computer Graphics (p5.js)

Origin (0,0) at top-left

y++ moves ↓ DOWN

Why? Computer graphics systems use top-left origin because:
  • Matches how we read (left to right, top to bottom)
  • Matches how monitors draw pixels (top to bottom scan lines)
  • Historical convention from early CRT displays
